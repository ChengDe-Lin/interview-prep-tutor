# Credibility Notes

這份是輔助 credibility，不要硬塞進 3-minute intro。當 Peter 問 leadership、team fit、executive communication、growth、career trajectory 的時候再用。

## 快速升遷 / Staff Trajectory

這個是 credibility signal，不是主故事。

## 可直接說的英文

> I was promoted to Senior Engineer in about two years, faster than the usual path in my company. More recently, my manager has also started discussing a Staff-level path for next year. I see that as a signal that my impact has moved beyond individual delivery: I have been owning platform-level problems, influencing other teams, and driving adoption across the organization.

## 為什麼有用

- 顯示 learning speed。
- 顯示你不是只做 assigned task，而是有 ownership。
- 顯示 manager 對你有信任。
- 可以支撐 Team Fit 和 Bias for Action。
- 可以暗示你已經在做 senior IC 以上的事情。

## 風險

- Staff promotion 還沒發生，不要說成保證。
- 不要講得像 entitlement。
- 除非 Peter 追問，不然一句帶過就好。

## 副董事長報告 / Executive Communication

這個對 Solutions Architect 很有價值。

Databricks SA 需要對不同層級的人講同一套 solution：

- Engineer 在意 architecture / implementation / trade-off。
- Data leader 在意 governance / reliability / platform direction。
- Business leader 在意 adoption / speed / cost / outcome。
- Executive 在意 strategic direction / why now。

你跟副董事長報告的經驗，可以證明你能把技術包裝成高層聽得懂的 business value。

## 可直接說的英文

> I also had the chance to represent our team and present this agentic workflow direction to our vice chairman. The important part was not to explain every technical detail, but to translate the work into business terms: how it reduces handoff, shortens product iteration, avoids stale design artifacts, and helps the organization move toward AI-native product development. That experience was valuable because it forced me to communicate the same architecture differently depending on the audience.

## 可以用在哪些題目

- Team Fit
- Customer Advisor / Pre-Sales
- Executive communication
- Influence without authority
- Driving adoption

## 連到 Databricks 的說法

> I think this maps well to Solutions Architect work, because the same solution has to make sense to different stakeholders: engineers, data teams, platform owners, and executives. The message has to change by audience, but the architecture and value story need to stay consistent.

## 如果被問：「你怎麼跟高層溝通？」

用副董事長報告這段。

## 如果被問：「你為什麼 ready for this role？」

用快速升遷 + platform ownership。

## 可直接說的英文

> My promotion path is one signal, but the stronger reason is that I have already been operating in a cross-team platform role: designing the architecture, getting adoption from other teams, and explaining the value to both engineers and executives.

## 如果被問：「你跟 pure software engineer 有什麼不同？」

## 可直接說的英文

> I still have strong engineering depth, but much of my recent impact has been in translating technical systems into workflows other people can adopt. That includes PMs, designers, engineers, managers, and executive stakeholders.

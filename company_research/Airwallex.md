# Airwallex — Singapore Research

## Company Overview

- **Singapore office**: Global headquarters is in Singapore (Tanjong Pagar district, tallest building in Singapore), opened February 2023. Singapore is not a branch — it is Airwallex's primary HQ alongside San Francisco (dual HQ established December 2025).
- **Office size / growth**: Local headcount expanded 62% in 2025; engineering roles alone grew 130% YoY. As of early 2026, the company has 2,000+ employees globally with Singapore being the largest hub outside Melbourne.
- **Teams based here**: Financial Platform, Global Treasury Payment Network, Data and AI Infrastructure, Clearing & Settlement, Spend Automation & AI Features, Tech Platform (backend infrastructure). Singapore engineers build core payment rails — not APAC-only features.
- **Core products relevant to Singapore**: Multi-currency accounts, global FX, cross-border payments, embedded finance APIs, spend management (cards + expense automation), and increasingly AI-powered financial operations. Revenue in Singapore grew 107% YoY (FY2025); transaction volume up 93%.

## Engineering Culture

- **Tech stack**: Java, Go, Rust, Lua, Python. Cloud: AWS, GCP, Aliyun. Infrastructure: Kubernetes, Docker, Istio. Data stores: PostgreSQL, Redis, Elasticsearch. Streaming: Kafka, Flink, Spark. AI tooling: LLM-powered internal tools (AirCheck — an LLM code reviewer that auto-detects bugs, typos, and unclear merge request descriptions).
- **Known engineering values**:
  - *Builder-first*: "Founder-like energy, grit, curiosity, problem-solving." Mindset over pedigree.
  - *Move fast with conviction*: Ship in weeks, not years. Product, engineering, and commercial teams co-build.
  - *Ownership end-to-end*: Engineers are expected to take a feature from design through production, not hand off.
  - *Deep technical craft*: Strong preference for engineers who can handle distributed systems complexity at scale (millions of daily transactions, financial integrity, full traceability).
  - *AI-native*: Generative AI team building LLM-driven automation for internal ops and customer-facing features; Analytics/AI team building predictive spend analytics.
- **Work style**: Collaborative, fast-paced. 80% employee pride rate, 93% understand how their work contributes (H1 2025 engagement survey). OKRs are transparent team-wide. Described as "fairly informal and laid back" in interview process.

## Interview Style

- **Process structure**:
  1. Recruiter screen (30 min) — role overview + background
  2. Technical screen — coding challenge (easy-to-medium LeetCode), background walkthrough
  3. Take-home OR live coding challenge (candidate's choice) — deeper technical assessment
  4. Final round with senior engineering leader (VP Eng / CTO level) — culture fit, values alignment, growth trajectory
  - Timeline: ~28 days average
- **Technical expectations**: Distributed systems knowledge important (they process millions of transactions/day with full financial traceability). System design for high-throughput, fault-tolerant payment systems. Knowledge of Java/Go microservices is valued. Data consistency and performance optimization questions common.
- **Known BQ themes**:
  - Cultural fit and values alignment (especially "builder" and "founder-like" mentality)
  - How you handle ambiguity and move fast with conviction
  - Examples of end-to-end ownership
  - Cross-functional collaboration (product + engineering + commercial)
  - How you've learned fast and iterated
- **Tone**: Informal and human. Airwallex explicitly designs interviews to be conversational. Candidates are encouraged to ask questions and assess the company in return.

## Customized Pitch Notes

- **Why Here emphasis**: Airwallex is at a rare inflection — triple-digit revenue growth, $8B valuation, 130% engineering growth in Singapore, and an explicit AI-first roadmap. This is not a "big stable company" hire — it is joining as a builder at the moment the company goes from regional challenger to global infrastructure. ChengDe's combination of fast shipping (agentic AI harness), systems depth (patents, NTU), and cross-team leverage (collaboration award) is exactly the "founder-like" profile Airwallex publicly states they hire for.
- **Angle to highlight**: Lead with the AI angle — Airwallex's internal AirCheck tool and their new Generative AI team signal that AI-native engineering is now a core priority. ChengDe's agentic AI harness story shows he has already done this at a production level, not just as a side project.
- **Singapore headquarters angle**: Airwallex's global HQ is in Singapore. Engineering decisions that affect the global product are made here. This is not a satellite office story — make that explicit in the "Why Here" section.
- **Watch out for**: Airwallex is pre-IPO and at hyper-growth stage. Interviewers will probe whether you can handle ambiguity and fast change. Avoid language that suggests you need process stability or heavy structure. Emphasize adaptability and bias to ship.
- **Avoid**: Over-indexing on academic credentials. Airwallex values "mindset over pedigree" — NTU 1st in class is worth one mention, but spend more time on *what you shipped* and *how fast* you grew.

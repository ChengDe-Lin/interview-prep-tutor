# Additional Singapore Target Research

> Research conducted 2026-04-09 after removing Shopee/Uber/Nvidia/Netflix from target list. Goal: find additional high-quality product engineering companies in Singapore to expand the target list.

## TL;DR — The Honest Finding

**Singapore doesn't have a huge hidden layer of high-paying product engineering companies beyond what's already on your list.** Most US tech companies use Singapore as APAC sales/solutions HQ, not product engineering. After checking 20+ additional candidates, only **4 meaningful adds** emerged.

Your existing list is already comprehensive. The anxiety you're feeling is reasonable — the SG product engineering market is genuinely smaller than the US or Sydney markets.

## Surprising Findings

1. **Jane Street trains OCaml on the job** — this changes the math completely. Combined with Python being first-class there, the language barrier is lower than expected.

2. **Figma has only ~21 employees in SG** — despite being a big name, the office is tiny and mostly customer-facing.

3. **Many US SaaS companies treat SG as APAC sales hub** — this is the recurring pattern. Canva, Atlassian, Snowflake, Datadog, Cloudflare, Figma all fit this description.

4. **DBS pay is lower than expected** — a major Singapore bank but SWE pay tops out at SGD 165K for Senior level. Traditional bank pay scale.

5. **Garena is separate enough from Shopee** that it may be worth a second look despite the parent Sea Ltd concerns.
